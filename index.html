We are a production company making creative documentaries and delivering digital stories across all platforms.

Petra Pan Film is a dynamic and ambitious international group of film professionals focusing on strong author's signature and high artistic profile in every field of filmmaking. Aiming to product innovative films with sense of humor. The company has strong ties to film financiers in the South East Balkan region as well as good relations to key partners on an international level.

Petra Pan Film is a Slovenian film production company founded in 2003 by director Petra Selishkar and director of photography Brand Ferro. The company produces creative documentaries and art house fiction films for Balkan and international market. Company's long term focus is developing creativity through education and promotion of documentary film.

Dunajska cesta 195
1131 Ljubljana, Slovenia

PPFP is a North Macedonian production company established in 2007 by Brand Ferro and Petra Selishkar. In 2017 producer Sara Ferro joined the team and manages the company. PPFP is one of the few creative documentary production companies in North Macedonia, focusing mainly on author driven and personal documentary films, but also feature films with strong author's signature. It specifically connects various countries to North Macedonia and Balkan countries in terms of coproduction and distribution.

Atinska ulica 21/24
1000 Skopje, N.Macedonia

In 2010, our team founded the association for promotion of creative documentary film MakeDox and initiated the creation of the MakeDox International Creative Documentary Film Festival, the Traveling Cinema in rural communities of North Macedonia and implemented an educational documentary program for high schools in the country.

With our films and activities we are finding the way to communicate with audience and express our vision on the world around us. We aim to create films beyound the norms of documentary film.
